Legal Assistance

Tesa Debt Recovery Management Limited offers legal assistance services aimed at helping businesses and individuals recover debts in compliance with the law. We work with experienced lawyers who specialize in debt collection, providing legal support to navigate the complexities of debt recovery procedures.

How We Help

As a trusted third-party service provider, we act as an intermediary between you and the legal processes involved in debt recovery. Our legal team is highly skilled in representing your interests, filing claims, negotiating settlements, and pursuing judgments to ensure that outstanding debts are paid.

Our Legal Services Include:

  • Debt Recovery Litigation: Our lawyers will take legal action against debtors in cases where informal collections have failed, including issuing court proceedings and attending hearings.
  • Settlement Negotiation: We assist in negotiating settlements between you and debtors to ensure quick resolution of outstanding debts.
  • Judgment Enforcement: Once a judgment is obtained, our legal team can enforce it through various legal measures such as wage garnishment, property liens, or asset seizure.
  • Debt Recovery Consultation: We provide expert advice on debt recovery matters, helping you understand the best course of action for your specific situation.
  • Credit Control and Risk Management: We assist businesses in managing credit risk and establishing preventive measures to avoid future payment issues.

At Tesa, we are committed to protecting your rights while ensuring the debt recovery process is handled efficiently and professionally. Whether you need legal representation or expert advice, our team of lawyers is here to provide the necessary legal support to resolve your debt recovery matters.
