Investigation Services

At Tesa Debt Recovery Management Limited, we specialize in conducting thorough investigations to identify debtors, uncover financial information, and gather evidence for debt recovery. Our expert investigators work efficiently and discreetly to uncover the truth behind outstanding debts, allowing you to take the necessary legal steps to recover funds.

How We Help

Our investigation services are designed to provide you with actionable insights into the whereabouts and financial status of debtors. We use advanced tools, techniques, and databases to track down individuals or companies responsible for unpaid debts. Whether it's locating missing debtors or investigating financial misconduct, Tesa ensures a comprehensive investigation process.

Types of Investigation Services We Offer:

  • Debtor Location: Identifying the whereabouts of debtors who have intentionally avoided payment.
  • Background Checks: Conducting thorough financial and personal background checks to assess the debtor's ability to repay debts.
  • Asset Investigation: Tracking down assets owned by debtors to facilitate the recovery process.
  • Fraud Investigation: Investigating cases of fraud, including identifying fraudulent transactions or asset concealment.

With our vast network and experience, Tesa ensures that every investigation is carried out professionally and within the boundaries of the law. Our team works with you to tailor the investigation to meet your specific needs.
